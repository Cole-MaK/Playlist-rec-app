What is this app?

This app takes one your your playlists to compare to either your Daylist or Discover Weekly to give you a ranking of songs that it thinks you will like the most.

How do I use this?

1. Sign into Spotify and authorizing
2. choose one of your playlists to use to help the algorithm give you recommendations
3. select either your daylist or discover weekly to look through
4. press the button :D
5. You want to make sure your daylist and discover weekly is shown on your profile by right clicking the playlist and selecting, "Add to Profile". It is best to select a song or playlist that has a similar genre to that of your daylist. If comparing to Discover weekly it does not matter.

Why do I need to Authorize

Authorization is required to get song information from your different playlists
